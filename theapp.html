The App

We are developing the all-in-one web- and mobile platform to empower young Europeans to engage with the European Union.

...app demo is coming soon...


The best part?

It centralises all EU related information.

And it is all community driven.

The Main Features

I. Central Source

Provide a central source of information about the European Union and our opportunities within it. Transparency is essential for trust.

II. Education

Past. Present. Future. Overcoming challenges. Powerful democracies are founded on education.

III. Decision-making

Connect Young People effectively and seamlessly with decision-makers and contribute to open-democracies. Communication is key.

IV. Communities

Empower Young People to form and join communities and translate their knowledge into real-world actions. Enhancing the European culture and fostering Europeanisation. Overcoming global challenges need collaborative action.

Our mobile platform contributes to realising the European Youth Goals as part of the EU Youth Strategy 2019-2027.

  • Provides

    them with continuously updated information about the EU and the opportunities within it

  • Equips

    them with knowledge about the EU and the pressing global challenges of the 21st Century, and teaches them how to help overcome them

  • Empowers

    them to share their ideas, participate in community events and build their social networks with other like-minded people

  • Enables

    them to contribute to decision-making processes and translate their knowledge directly into action as part of their communities

We believe that a powerful, democratic Europe is founded on education.

That's why our app has a core focus on it.
