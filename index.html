Main features:

I. Central Source

Provide a central source of information about the European Union and our opportunities within it. Transparency is essential for trust.

II. Education

Past. Present. Future. Overcoming challenges. Powerful democracies are founded on education.

III. Decision-making

Connect Young People effectively and seamlessly with decision-makers and contribute to open-democracies. Communication is key.

IV. Communities

Empower Young People to form and join communities and translate their knowledge into real-world actions. Enhancing the European culture and fostering Europeanisation. Overcoming global challenges need collaborative action.

Our mobile platform contributes to realising the European Youth Goals as part of the EU Youth Strategy 2019-2027.

Who We Are

Our goal is to engage Young Citizens with the European Union to actively shape its future.
