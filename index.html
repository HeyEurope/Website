Our Goals

Briefly?
Enabling the EU to be more democratic.

Central Source

Provide a central source of information about the European Union and our opportunities within it. Provide a platform to contribute to decision-making processes within the EU.

Education

Educate Young people on both local(EU wide)- and global challenges and how they can contribute to overcoming them. Every individual counts.

Connect Young People effectively and seamlessly with decision-makers and contribute to open-democracies.
Empower Young People to form and join communities and translate their knowledge into real-world actions.

Our mobile platform contributes to realising the European Youth Goals as part of the EU Youth Strategy 2019-2027.

Our Values

  • Transparency

    When we understand, we can improve.

  • Open Education

    We can only sustain strong democracies if we are taught how to.

  • Strong Communities

    Communities sustain the lasting changes.

  • Involvement in decision-making

    We can only make universally good decisions when we listen to everyone.
