The Goal

Our goal is to

  • provide a central source of information about the EU and the opportunities within it
  • educate Young people on how they can contribute to the EU and its contemporary issues
  • involve their ideas in the decision-making processes
  • empower them to form and join communities and translate their knowledge into real-world actions
