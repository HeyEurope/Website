Help Us

Hi!
We are HeyEurope, a non-profit student-led project which aims to enable young Europeans to understand the European Union and unlock its full potential. Our vision is to provide a network of digital channels where we can effectively reach young people to increase transparency and trust towards the EU. Furthermore, we want to educate on EU related matters and the opportunities it offers, allowing them to connect with these opportunities and positively affect the European Union, helping it achieve its goals.

We are remotely working volunteers ourselves, therefore we are looking for other volunteers to join our team, regardless of their age, experience level or country of origin.
