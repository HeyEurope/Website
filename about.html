About Us

We are a student led non-profit project with a mission of empowering young Europeans to accelerate Europe's transition into sustainability.

We are a diverse team of members from all around Europe.

We believe that the European Union has loads of potential to make our lives happier and more sustainable, that is why we embarked on the journey of HeyEurope.

Our Goals

  1. 1 become the #1 central provider of all EU-related information

  2. 2 connect young people with all the available opportunities offered by the EU, enable them to contribute to decision-making processes and help the EU achieve its goals

  3. 3 inspire young people for positive action through education, and connect them together from all around the Europe to flourish the creation of socially and environmentally regenerative projects within the EU
