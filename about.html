About Us

Hello!
We are HeyEurope, a non-profit student-led organisation that aims to enable young Europeans to understand the European Union and unlock its full potential.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a network of digital channels where we can effectively reach young people to increase transparency and trust towards the European Union, educate them on EU related matters, connect them with people from all around Europe and empower them to live with the opportunities offered by the EU and thus accelerate it towards achieving its aims.

These channels will first include tools already existing like Instagram, YouTube, Discord, Spotify ... and much more!

Through these we are going to provide our community with a centralised source of information to different EU related news - EuroNews, EU Institutions/Agencies & their socials | events - Conference on the Future of Europe & much more | opportunities - study, work, travel, decision-making... Erasmus+, European Solidarity Corps, EuroDesk & much more!

We are a diverse, multicultural team of members from all around Europe.
We formed our core team during the SOLVE-THE-GAP-48h Hackathon back in March 2021. Since, we have been continuously expanding our team and working to achieve our mission, all remotely.

We believe that the European Union has loads of potential to improve our well-being and ensure a safe and just future for all of us, this is why we embarked on the journey of HeyEurope.

Our Goals

  1. 1 become the central provider of the largest selection of EU related information for young Europeans

  2. 2 connect young people with all the available opportunities offered by the EU, enable them to contribute to decision-making processes and help the EU achieve its goals

  3. 3 inspire young people for positive action through education, and connect them together from all around the Europe to flourish the creation of socially and environmentally regenerative projects within the EU
